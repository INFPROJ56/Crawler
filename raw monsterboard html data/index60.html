Project Leader Method Development

Plaats: Noord-Brabant, NB
Referentie: 20007953 (IGB 18245495)


Functieomschrijving

In this job you can combine your project management skills, your expertise in analytical chemistry and you'll have contact with external clients. For an international company Balans Laboratorium is looking for a

Project Leader Method Development

You'll be responsible for the scientific studies for different clients regarding method development of UPLC, GC, LC and ICP, all in combination with MS or MSMS. Your job is to oversee the performance, monitor the quality and interpret and report the results. You have contact with the clients or in case of multiple projects with one client, you'll have contact with a project manager. You may also be asked to join business development with the acquisition of new clients.
You will work in a team of three study leaders and in case of absence of the team manager, you will replace him. We are therefore seeking a senior candidate (not a PhD starter).

Locatie/Standplaats

Noord-Brabant

Functie-eisen
  • A PhD in Analytical Chemistry:
  • Several years of working experience after PhD:
  • Experience with abovementioned techniques. At least with LCMS, more is preferred:
  • Experience with GLP is a plus:
  • Good Project Management skills:
  • Fluent in English. You are obliged to learn the Dutch language:
  • Good communication skills.
