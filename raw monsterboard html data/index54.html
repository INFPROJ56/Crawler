Territory Manager (Field Sales)

Ritchie Bros. Auctioneers has an immediate opening for a field sales professional to serve our customers as a Territory Manager (Field Sales) in the Benelux Region, ideally based out of the Southern part of Holland. 
 
Advantages of Applying:


  • Competitive base salary.
  • Performance-based bonus on target achievements – excellent performance is rewarded.
  • Comprehensive health insurance coverage.
  • Corporate pension plan with company matching.
  • Employee Share Purchase Plan with company matching.
  • Company car, laptop, and smart phone.
  • Ongoing training and professional development.
  • A rewarding career that is stable and long-term with growth potential.

General Accountability:
The Territory Manager will play an integral role to execute Ritchie Bros. Auctioneers’ Corporate Strategic Sales Plan in generating consignments through sales and marketing efforts by attracting buyers and sellers to the Ritchie Bros. sales and promoting the Company’s unreserved auction services and programs to existing and new customers within their assigned territory.
 
Duties and Responsibilities

 


  • Introduce, promote and sell Ritchie Bros. services and programs to current and future customers through prospecting, cold calling and account maintenance activities.
  • Encourage potential buyers and sellers to come out to our auctions.
  • Assess customers’ needs and provide solutions based on our service offerings.
  • Assist with equipment appraisals. We’re not brand loyal at Ritchie Bros. -- you'll be able to work with it all.
  • Take a break from the road and work at the auction, where you can drive gear, catch bids and network with your customers and colleagues all in one place.
  • Monitor the market and your competitors' services, prices, auctions and sales.
  • Represent Ritchie Bros. at professional association conferences and trade shows.


Working Conditions:   

 

 


  • Attend 5 auction sales, 1 to 2 days each in duration within the region, where you'll meet with your colleagues and customers.
  • Travel is required to cover the territory and prior to each auction sale.
  • Work primarily from a home office and on the road.


 

Qualifications & Requirements

 


  • A Business Studies degree in Business Management or Commerce is considered an asset.
  • Fluency in English and Dutch is required, plus strong communication skills (written and oral), especially when it comes to listening to customers.
  • A minimum of 5 years' experience in a structured outside sales role, and a consistent track-record of meeting or exceeding sales quotas.
  • Experience around construction equipment is a considerable asset for this role.
  • A drive to seek new business through prospecting and cold-calling.
  • A high level of competitiveness without ever sacrificing integrity to win business.
  • An aptitude for numbers and strong presentation, strategic thinking, negotiation and closing skills.
  • A natural ability at building and maintaining authentic customer relationships.
  • A passport, a valid driver’s license and a clean driver's abstract.
